Graded English Medium School

In 1984, GEMS School was established in Sanepa, Lalitpur with 78 students and 25 staff members. Initially, it offered classes from Nursery to grade III, but it has now grown into a higher secondary school with classes from grade one to twelve. The school recently completed construction on its own school complex in Dhapakhel, which is 1.5 kilometers away from Sato Dobato point on the Ring road. In August 2000, the school shifted all classes from Kiran Bhawan in Sanepa to the new complex, accommodating classes from four to ten. From 2077, the school started running classes from one to ten in the new complex.

About GEMS

GEMS School boasts excellent infrastructure, complete with state-of-the-art facilities. The academic buildings are modern and include spacious classrooms, laboratories, a conference hall, administrative offices, and a cafeteria. Additionally, the school has spacious playing grounds for football, basketball, and other sports. There are separate buildings and rooms dedicated to extra-curricular activities, such as gymnastics, judo, and music lessons for various instruments like the keyboard, percussion, guitar, violin, and sitar.

Services
